About Us

We are a small, family run memorial masonry business based in Garforth but serving all areas of Leeds including Bramley, Pudsey, Morley, Rothwell and Horsforth. With over 39 years experience in the memorial trade, we pride ourselves on our exceptional reputation for offering a compassionate and professional memorial service and providing the highest quality of workmanship to all our customers.

To talk to us at G.B Newell & Sons Ltd about your individual requirements for memorials, plaques, headstone vases and renovations throughout Leeds and the surrounding areas, contact us directly today. We are happy to accommodate local home visits by arrangement and we are proud members of the British Register of Approved Memorial Masons (BRAMM).
